How participation works on APEX IPO ACCESS for the SpaceX IPO1

Apex IPO Access connects qualified investors worldwide with curated pre-IPO opportunities in the most sought-after late-stage private companies — SpaceX, Stripe, OpenAI, Anthropic, and others.

1) Apply for Access

  1. Create your account at apexipoaccess.com and submit your personal, contact, and investor profile information.
  2. Choose your account type — Individual, Joint, IRA, or Trust.
  3. Submit your application for compliance review.

2) Compliance Review & Account Activation

  1. Our compliance team reviews each application — typically within 1 business day.
  2. You will receive an email notification once your account is approved.
  3. Approved investors gain full access to the investment dashboard.

3) Verify Your Email & Complete KYC

  1. Click the verification link sent to your registered email address.
  2. Upload a government-issued photo ID and proof of address.
  3. KYC verification is required by global financial regulations.

4) Fund Your Account

  1. Bank Wire (USD): Wire instructions are provided in your dashboard. Funds typically clear within 1 business day.
  2. Cryptocurrency: BTC, ETH, USDT, and USDC accepted. Confirmed and credited automatically.

5) Choose Your Investment Plan

Each investment is held for a fixed tenure with a defined target return range based on historical performance of comparable pre-IPO transactions:

Lock-up Period Target Return Range
3 Months 35% – 60%
6 Months 40% – 70%
12 Months 50% – 80%

6) Track Your Portfolio

  1. Monitor active investments, accrued returns, and lock-up countdown in real time.
  2. Receive email notifications for key events: deposits credited, allocations confirmed, payouts processed.
  3. Access your full transaction history at any time.

7) Withdraw Earnings After Lock-up

  1. At the end of your selected tenure, principal plus returns become available in your withdrawal balance.
  2. Withdraw via bank wire or to your registered cryptocurrency wallet.
  3. Re-invest at any time to compound returns.

1 Apex IPO Access provides access to pre-IPO investment opportunities. Target return ranges are based on historical performance of comparable transactions and are not guaranteed. All investments carry risk including possible loss of principal. Pre-IPO and private placement investments are illiquid and locked for the chosen tenure. Apex IPO Access is open to qualified investors globally; specific opportunities may be restricted by jurisdiction. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Please review all offering documents before investing.
