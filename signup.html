What type of account do you want to open?

Choose the account that best fits your investment goals. You can open more than one.

Individual brokerage account

A standard taxable account for one person to invest in stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, and pre-IPO opportunities.

Joint brokerage account

An account shared between two adults — ideal for couples or business partners investing together.

Traditional or Roth IRA

Tax-advantaged retirement account. Choose Traditional for tax-deferred growth or Roth for tax-free withdrawals.

Trust or entity account

For trusts, LLCs, corporations, and other legal entities investing in private markets.

SIPC
Brokerage accounts are protected up to $500,000 by SIPC. Apex IPO Access is a member of SIPC.
