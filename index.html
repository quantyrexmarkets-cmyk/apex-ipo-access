COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

Am I eligible to participate?

Apex IPO Access is open to verified accredited investors as defined by SEC Rule 501. Verification typically takes under 10 minutes through our compliance partner.

What is the minimum allocation?

Minimums vary by offering and tier. SpaceX secondary allocations typically start at $25,000, while reserved IPO positions begin at $10,000 for our Premier tier members.

How are my shares custodied?

All shares are held in your name through our regulated broker-dealer custodian. Funds in transit are kept in segregated escrow accounts at FDIC-insured institutions.

When does the SpaceX IPO window open?

Current intelligence projects a 2026–2027 listing window. Members receive real-time updates on filing milestones, roadshow signals, and allocation deadlines.

What fees does APEX charge?

A flat annual membership fee plus a transparent allocation fee on executed positions. No carry, no hidden spreads. Full fee schedule provided at onboarding.

Can I exit my position before the IPO?

Yes. Secondary liquidity is available through our partner marketplaces, subject to lock-up terms specific to each offering. Your dashboard tracks all liquidity windows in real time.
